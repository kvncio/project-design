Saudi Arabi 

Saudi Arabia (or officially: the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia) is an Arab country  
and is the largest country in the Middle East in area. It is located specifically in the southwest of the continent of Asia
and constitutes the largest part of the Arabian Peninsula, with an area of ​​about two million square kilometers. It is bordered to the north by the Republic of Iraq and Jordan, and by the State of Kuwait to the northeast, and to the east by the State of Qatar and the United Arab Emirates, in addition to the Kingdom of Bahrain, which is linked to Saudi Arabia through the King Fahd Causeway located on the waters of the Arabian Gulf
and to the south it is bordered by Yemen, and the Sultanate of Oman to the south. East, and it is bordered by the Red Sea to the west.
