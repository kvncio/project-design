Riyadh

Riyadh is the capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
It is its largest city and the third largest Arab capital in terms of population
Riyadh is located in the middle of the Arabian Peninsula on the Najd Plateau , at an altitude of 600 meters above sea level . 
It is the headquarters of the Emirate of the Riyadh Region, according to the administrative division of the Saudi regions.
